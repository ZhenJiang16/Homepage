--- title: "**Project (b): Li-Air Batteries**" excerpt: "In this project, we focused on the atomic mechanisms and associated thermodynamic overpotential of discharging/charging reactions on Mo3P and Co3O4 electrodes. Two different discharging pathways (solution and surface pathway) were investigated in high (DMSO) and low (CH3CN) donor number solvents. The meachanism and kientic behavior of interfacial redox reactions and discharge product transportation principles were studied in explicit aprotic solvent and most thermodynamically favourable electrode facets.
**Methods**: Ab initio thermodynamics, DFT-derived overpotentials, ab initio molecular dynamics (Born Openheimer molecular dynamics), ab initio Grand Canonical Monte Calro, slow-growth simulation, blue-moon ensemble, Einstein diffusion equation, density of states, band structures, HOMO-LUMO.
**Software package**: VASP, p4vasp, vaspkit, Travis, VESTA, VMD, Inkscape, Latex, imovie.
**Publications**: *J. Phys. Chem. C* 125(40), 21873-21881 (**2021**); *Adv. Mater.* 2004028 (**2020**)" collection: portfolio ---
