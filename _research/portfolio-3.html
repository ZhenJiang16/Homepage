--- title: "Project (b): Li-air Batteries" excerpt: "In this project, the atomic mechanisms and associated thermodynamic overpotential of discharging/charging reactions on Mo3P and Co3O4electrodes. Two different discharging pathways (solution and surface pathway) are investigated in high (DMSO) and low (CH3CN) donor number solvents. The meachanism and kientic behavior of interfacial redox reactions and discharge product transportation principles are studied in explicit aprotic solvent and most thermodynamic favourable electrode facets.
**Methods**: Computational Lithium electrode based thermodynamic free energy calculations, DFT-derived overpotentials, ab initio molecular dynamics (Born Openheimer molecular dynamics), slow growth simulation, blue-moon ensemble, Einstein diffusion equation, static DFT, density of states, band structures, HOMO-LUMO.
**Software package**: VASP, p4vasp, vaspkit, Travis, VESTA, VMD, Inkscape, Latex, moviemaker.
**Publications**: *J. Phys. Chem. C* 125(40), 21873-21881 (**2021**); *Adv. Mater.* 2004028 (**2020**)" collection: portfolio ---
