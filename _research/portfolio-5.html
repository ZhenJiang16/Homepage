--- title: "**Project (d): Electrocatalysis**" excerpt: "I am also experienced in the atomic simulations of electrocatalysis, including hydrogen evolution reactions (HER), oxygen evolution/reduction reactions (OER/ORR), and CO2 reduction reactions (CO2RR). The mechansitic study together with free energy calculations based on computational hydrogen electrode model are performed. I investigated the overpotentials of ORR on the FeN4-dopped graphene with a family of face-center cubic (fcc) metal supports. More ab initio calculations are being performed to identify effecient catalysts for HER, OER, ORR, and CO2RR.
**Methods**: Ab initio thermodynamics, DFT-derived overpotentials, electron density analysis, d band center, surface energy, lattice mismatch.
**Software package**: VASP, p4vasp, vaspkit, VESTA, Inkscape, Latex.
